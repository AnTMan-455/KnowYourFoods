Packaged Fruit Juices

Packaged fruit juices are drinks sold in cartons, bottles, or boxes that contain fruit juice or fruit-flavored beverages. While they may taste similar to fresh juice, many packaged juices contain added sugars, preservatives, and flavorings.

What are Packaged Fruit Juices?

Packaged fruit juices are beverages made from fruit concentrates, flavorings, and sometimes added sugars. Although they may contain some vitamins, many versions have less fiber and more sugar than whole fruits.

Tip: Eating whole fruits instead of drinking juice provides fiber and helps you feel fuller.

Health Considerations

Packaged fruit juices can be enjoyed occasionally, but frequent consumption may increase sugar intake. Choosing fresh fruits and water most of the time can support healthier daily habits.

Balanced eating tip: Try replacing packaged juice with fresh fruit and water.
