Fish Balls / Kikiam (Deep Fried)

Fish balls and kikiam are popular street foods often deep-fried and served with sweet or spicy sauces. While they are affordable and flavorful snacks, they are often made from processed fish mixtures and cooked in large amounts of oil.

🧈 Deep Fried
⚙️ Processed Fish
🧂 High Sodium
🔥 Moderate Calories
✅ Better Choice: Grilled Fish Snacks

What are Fish Balls and Kikiam?

Fish balls and kikiam are commonly sold by street vendors and are made from ground fish mixed with flour and seasonings. They are typically deep-fried and served on sticks with dipping sauces. While they provide protein, the frying process adds extra oil and calories.

Health Considerations

Fish balls and kikiam can be enjoyable street snacks, but frequent consumption of deep-fried foods may increase fat intake. Eating them occasionally and balancing meals with vegetables and whole foods can help maintain healthier eating habits.

Balanced eating tip: Limit fried street foods and choose grilled or fresh foods more often.
