Processed Ham & Bacon

Processed meats like ham and bacon are popular breakfast and sandwich ingredients. They are preserved using curing, smoking, or salting methods. While flavorful, they often contain high amounts of sodium, saturated fat, and preservatives.

What are Processed Ham and Bacon?

Ham and bacon are meats that have been cured, smoked, or salted to extend their shelf life and enhance flavor. They are commonly used in sandwiches, breakfasts, and fast foods. However, these products are considered processed meats because they contain added preservatives and salt.

Health Considerations

Processed meats like ham and bacon can be enjoyed occasionally, but frequent consumption may increase sodium and saturated fat intake. Balancing meals with fresh protein sources and vegetables can support healthier eating habits.

Balanced eating tip: If eating bacon or ham, try pairing it with vegetables and whole foods instead of additional processed foods.
