Pancakes with Syrup

Pancakes are a popular breakfast food made from flour, eggs, milk, and sugar. They are often served with syrup, butter, or sweet toppings. While they are tasty and comforting, pancakes with syrup can contain a large amount of sugar and refined carbohydrates.

🍬 High Sugar (Syrup)
🍞 Refined Flour
🧈 Added Butter
🔥 Moderate–High Calories
✅ Better Choice: Whole Grain Pancakes

What are Pancakes?

Pancakes are flat cakes cooked on a griddle or pan using a batter made from flour, eggs, milk, and baking powder. They are commonly eaten with syrup, butter, whipped cream, or fruit toppings. Many commercial syrups contain added sugars and artificial flavors.

Health Considerations

Pancakes can be enjoyed occasionally, but frequent consumption with sugary syrups may increase sugar intake. Choosing whole grain pancakes and adding fruits instead of heavy syrups can provide a more balanced breakfast.

Balanced eating tip: Pair pancakes with eggs or yogurt to add protein and balance the meal.
