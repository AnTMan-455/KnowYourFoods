Candy

Candy is a sweet confection made primarily from sugar or syrup. It includes products such as gummy candies, hard candies, lollipops, and chewy sweets. While candy is enjoyable as a treat, it usually provides little nutritional value and contains high levels of sugar.

What is Candy?

Candy is a category of sweets made mainly from sugar, corn syrup, flavorings, and food coloring. It can come in many forms such as gummies, hard candies, caramel, or chewy sweets. Because candy contains mostly sugar, it provides quick energy but very few essential nutrients.

Tip: Eating fruit when craving sweets can provide natural sweetness along with vitamins and fiber.

Health Considerations

Candy is best enjoyed occasionally because frequent consumption may contribute to high sugar intake and dental problems. Maintaining balanced meals and limiting sugary snacks can support better overall health.

Balanced eating tip: Save candy for occasional treats and focus on healthier snacks most of the time.
