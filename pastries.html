Pastries (Croissants & Donuts)

Pastries such as croissants and donuts are baked or fried foods made with refined flour, sugar, butter, and oils. They are popular breakfast or snack items but are often high in calories, sugar, and saturated fat.

What are Pastries?

Pastries include foods such as croissants, donuts, danishes, and sweet baked goods. Croissants are made with layers of butter and dough, while donuts are usually fried and coated with sugar or glaze. These foods are delicious but are often considered treats rather than everyday staple foods.

Health Considerations

Pastries can be enjoyed as treats, but eating them frequently may increase sugar and saturated fat intake. Choosing healthier breakfast options such as whole grains, fruits, and protein sources can provide longer-lasting energy and better nutrition.

Balanced eating tip: Pair pastries with protein sources like eggs or yogurt to balance your meal.
