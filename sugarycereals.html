Sugary Cereals

Sugary cereals are breakfast cereals that contain added sugar to enhance flavor. They are often marketed to children and can taste sweet and appealing, but many varieties contain large amounts of sugar and refined grains.

What are Sugary Cereals?

Sugary cereals are breakfast cereals that contain added sugars, flavorings, and sometimes artificial colors. While they are convenient and widely consumed, many of these cereals contain more sugar than whole grain alternatives.

Tip: Choosing cereals with lower sugar and higher fiber can help create a healthier breakfast.

Health Considerations

Sugary cereals can be enjoyable occasionally, but frequent consumption may contribute to high sugar intake. Choosing whole grain cereals and adding fresh fruits can provide a more balanced and nutritious breakfast.

Balanced eating tip: Look for cereals with less than 10g of sugar per serving.
