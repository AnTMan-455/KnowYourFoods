Instant Noodles

Instant noodles are quick and convenient meals that are widely consumed around the world. They are usually made from refined wheat noodles and flavoring packets. While they are affordable and easy to prepare, they are often high in sodium and may lack essential nutrients.

⚙️ Highly Processed
✅ Better Choice: Add Vegetables

What are Instant Noodles?

Instant noodles are pre-cooked noodles that are dried and packaged with seasoning packets. They only require hot water or boiling to prepare. Many people enjoy them because they are fast and inexpensive, but the seasoning packets usually contain large amounts of sodium.

Tip: Adding vegetables, eggs, or lean meat to instant noodles can improve the nutritional value.

Health Considerations

Instant noodles can be convenient, but frequent consumption may lead to excessive sodium intake. Eating them occasionally and combining them with vegetables and protein sources can help create a more balanced meal.

Balanced eating tip: Try using only part of the seasoning packet to reduce sodium.
