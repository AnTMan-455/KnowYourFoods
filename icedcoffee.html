Iced Coffee with Syrup

Iced coffee with syrup is a cold coffee beverage often mixed with milk, cream, and flavored syrups such as vanilla, caramel, or chocolate. While refreshing, the added syrups and sweeteners can significantly increase sugar and calorie content.

What is Iced Coffee with Syrup?

Iced coffee is brewed coffee served over ice, often mixed with milk or cream. Many coffee drinks include flavored syrups to enhance sweetness and taste. However, these syrups may contain large amounts of added sugar.

Health Considerations

Coffee itself contains antioxidants and can be part of a balanced diet, but sweet syrups and creamers can add large amounts of sugar. Choosing less sweet versions may help reduce calorie intake.

Balanced eating tip: Try iced coffee with minimal syrup or natural milk for a lighter option.
