Shawarma

Shawarma is a popular wrap made with sliced meat, vegetables, and sauces inside flatbread. It can be flavorful and filling, but shawarma with lots of mayo is usually higher in calories, fat, and sodium because of the extra sauce.

✅ Better Choice: Less Mayo + More Veggies

What is Shawarma?

Shawarma is a wrap commonly filled with seasoned meat such as chicken or beef, along with vegetables like lettuce, onions, and tomatoes. It is often finished with sauces such as garlic sauce or mayonnaise. When a shawarma has a lot of mayo, the taste becomes richer, but the meal also becomes much heavier in fat and calories.

Health Considerations

Shawarma can provide protein and be satisfying, but versions with lots of mayo may contain a large amount of fat and sodium. Eating it occasionally is fine, but choosing more vegetables and less sauce can make it a better everyday option.

Balanced eating tip: Try shawarma with less mayo, more vegetables, and water instead of sugary drinks to make the meal lighter.
